Steep Slope Winery

Hand-Crafted: A step by step guide to our wine-making technique

At the Steep Slope winery, the grapes lead us through the cultivation process. Horticulturalists tend to the plants one by one with nothing but hand snips and their judgement. Since the natural world cannot be rushed, a new phase only begins once the plants indicate they are ready.

Cabernet Sauvignon

A sophisticated red with black current, leather, and oak flavors. We recommend pairing with strong flavors such as dark chocolate and beef. 14% Alcohol.

Merlot

Poppy's Portie Port

Seasonal Cycle

  • November – December: Rest after the Harvest.
  • January: Training the vines.
  • February – April: Nurturing the buds.
  • May – Mid-July: Hand selection of the grape clusters.
  • Mid-July – August: Ripening.
  • Septemebr – October: Testing and harvest.
